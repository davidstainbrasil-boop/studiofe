# Studio User Guide

Welcome to the CursosTecno Video Studio! This guide will help you create professional videos using our AI-powered tools.

## The Interface

### 1. Timeline & Layers
at the bottom of the screen is the timeline. This is where you compose your video.
- **Layers**: Stack media (Images, Text, Subtitles) on top of each other.
- **Duration**: Drag the edges of clips to trim or extend them.
- **Transitions**: Add smooth transitions between clips.

### 2. Live Preview
The center stage shows a real-time preview of your video.
- **Play/Pause**: Review your work instantly.
- **Seek**: Click anywhere on the progress bar to jump to that frame.

### 3. Notifications (New!)
Stay updated without leaving the page.
- **Bell Icon**: Located in the top-right corner.
- **Red Badge**: Indicates unread messages (e.g., "Render Completed").
- **Alerts**: We'll notify you when your export is ready or if there's an issue.

## Features

### AI Avatars
Create talking avatars from text.
1. Click **+ Add Media** > **AI Avatar**.
2. Select an avatar and background.
3. Enter your script.
4. Click **Generate**.

### Auto-Subtitles
1. Select your audio/video track.
2. Click **Generate Subtitles** in the properties panel.
3. We'll verify and align the text automatically.

## Exporting
Ready to share?
1. Click the **Export** button in the top bar.
2. Choose your quality (720p, 1080p).
3. We'll process it in the cloud. You can close the tab—we'll email you (and ping the bell!) when it's done.

## Need Help?
Contact support via the **?** icon in the dashboard.
